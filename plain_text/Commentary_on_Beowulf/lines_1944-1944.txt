Hemminges mǣg = Offa; in 1961 = Ēomēr. Was Hemming a brother of Gārmund? Or Gārmund's (or Offa's) father-in-law? (Cp. Nīðhādes mǣg, Wald. ii 8.) The name occurs in Ags., ON., and OHG. See Suchier, Beitr. iv 511 f.; Sievers, ib. x 501 f.; Binz 172; Bjorkman L 4.31.4.167 f. There is a village named Hemmingstedt in the southwestern part of Schleswig.
