 The hanging of the walls with tapestries is in conformity with Scand. and Ags. (also German) custom. See Montelius 150; Kålund and Guðmundsson, P. Grdr.2 iii 432, 477; Guþrúnarkv. ii 15; Tupper's Riddles, p. 194; Hel. 4544 f. Müller L 9.28.65. A close parallel to this particular instance is found Æneid i 637 ff. (Arch. cxxvi 342.)
