 The supplied readings are of course conjectural, but there are sufficient grounds for believing that they fairly represent the context. (forþ nē)h gefe(al)g/hæðnum horde, 'he made his way forwards near to the heathen hoard'; cp. 745, 2289 f. To judge from the facsimile, the MS. reading gefeng (so Holthausen, Schücking, Chambers) is by no means certain. -- 2217. nē hē þæt syððan (bemāð), 'nor did he [the dragon] afterwards conceal it,' i.e. he showed it very plainly. For the use of þ（ēah) 2218, see 1102.
