Nō ic on niht gefrægn etc. Prepositional phrases or adverbs of time and place modifying the object of the verb gefrignan or the infinitive phrase dependent on it, are placed before gefrignan; so 74, 2484, 2694, 2752, 2773. (Cf. Sievers, Beitr. xii 191.) See also 1197 (hȳran). The case is modified and complicated by the addition of the element of variation: 1 f.
