Ic wæs syfanwintre etc. On the custom (practised with especial frequency in Scandinavia) of placing children in the homes of others for their education, see F. Roeder, Über die Erziehung der vornehmen ags. Jugend in fremden Hausern, 1910; cf. L. M. Larson, JEGPh. xi 141-43. The training of youths was supposed to begin at the age of seven cf. Grimm R.A. 411. In the case of Bede we have his own testimony: mid pȳ ic wæs seofanwintre, pā wæs ic mid gīmene mīnra māga seald tō fēdanne ond tō lǣrenne pām ārwyrþan abbude Benedicie ond Cēolferþe æfter þon, OE. Bede 480.25 ff. (=v, c. 24).
