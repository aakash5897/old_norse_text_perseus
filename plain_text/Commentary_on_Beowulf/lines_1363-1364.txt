hrinde (bearwas). The epithet is eminently suitable symbolically; cp. hrīmige bearwas, Blickl. Hom. 209.32, on ðǣm isgean bearwum, ib. 35. (See Intr. lxi.) It is not to be inferred that Bēowulf found the trees covered with hoar-frost. He would not have sailed for Denmark in winter (see 1130 ff.).
