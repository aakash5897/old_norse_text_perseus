Him on fyrste gelomp/ǣdre mid yldum. The work was done quickly (ǣdre), considering the magnitude of the undertaking; on fyrste 'in due time' (cf. B.-T. Suppi.: first; not to be rendered, with Schü. Bd. 26 ff., by 'speedily'). The rapid construction of the hall seems to be one of the folk-tale elements of the story, cf. Panzer 257 n. 1. -- mid yldum, a formula-like expletive, see Intr. lxvii.
