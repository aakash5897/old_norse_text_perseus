Ne gefrægen ic þā mǣgþe māran weorode . . . sēl gebǣran. ran. A combination of two types, viz. a) ne hȳrde ic cȳmlīcor cēol gegyrwan 38 (1027, 1197, 1842); b) ðā ic wīde gefrægn weorc gebannan 74 (2484, 2694, 2752, 2773). Accordingly, þā is adverb. -- sēl gebæran; i.e., they behaved properly, as the occasion required, cp. Finnsb. 38. The reference here is to the etiquette (cp. fægere 1014) or to the splendid appearance of the retainers on the festive occasion (cp. Nibel. 593: swie wol man dā gebārte).
