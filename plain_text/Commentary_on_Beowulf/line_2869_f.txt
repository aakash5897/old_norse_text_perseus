swylce hē þrȳðlīcost/ōwer feor oððe nēah findan meahte. prȳðlīcost is left uninflected ; it may be said to agree, theoretically, with an indefinite object 'it.' Only partial parallels are 3161 f., Jul. 571 if. The change of d to ð appears imperative þrydlice found in Byrhtferð's Enchiridzon, Angl. viii 302. 14 is doubtful as to form and meaning.
