weotena dōme. A noteworthy allusion to the authority of the king's advisory council. Cp. Jul. 98: ofer witena dōm. King Ælfred undertook the codification of the laws 'mid mīnra witena geðeahte,' Ælfr. Laws, Introd. 49.9. Cf. F. Purlitz, Konig u. Witenagemot bei den Angelsachsen, Leipzig Diss., 1892, F. Liebermann, The National Assembly in the Anglo-Saxon Period, Halle a.S., 1913.
