folcum gefrǣge, 'famous among peoples.' The same use of the dative after foremǣrost, 309. -- fæder ellor hwearf (type D4). Note the periphrasis for 'dying' (Intr. lxv). The insertion of a comma (aldor, of earde) has not been deemed advisable in cases of this kind; cp., e.g., 36a: mǣrne be mæste, 140a, 213a, 265a, 420a, etc.
