þæt þe Sǣ-Gēatas sēlran næbben . . . Several edd. (thus Schücking, Sedgefield, Chambers) write þē; but the construction of the dat. (instr.) with a compar. (' better than you') is found nowhere else in Beowulf: The corresponding passage, 858ff. supports þæt þe; cp. 1846. (Arch. cxxvi 356 n.1.)
